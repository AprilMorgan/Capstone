Homeowner Tips!

Each month, on this page, we feature tips for homeowners!

Owning a home can be stressful, especially if something goes wrong.

To make it a bit easier and save time, here are a few helpful tips!

Tip of the Week!

Leaky water heaters pose several problems due to drain and relief valve leaks.

Replacing them is easy, but if the leak is coming from the tank instead, that's a serious issue.

The puddle means the glass on the inside of the tank has cracked and water is leaking out.

If not addressed, the tank will rupture and cause immediate flooding. Be sure to replace it!

In the event of a power outage, use a bucket of water to pour

into the toilet so you may still flush the toilet.

Insulation is rated by R or resistance value. The higher the number,

the better the insulation.

When out of town for a few days or more, turn off the main water

valve to avoid potential leaks.
